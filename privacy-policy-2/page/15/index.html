    Outstanding Traits And Their Benefits

    Powerful Grind

    The Baratza Virtuoso Burr Coffee Grinder is equipped with a powerful, high torque direct current motor.

    That effortlessly generates the rotational force which eventually crushes the coffee.

    It does extremely slow and so quietly that almost no noise is dissipated to the external environment.

    This permits long crushing cycles that ensure that the grains of the final output are as minute and fine as possible.

    The grains thus exude a smooth texture and great flavor.

    The quality of the final output does not, therefore, vary significantly from those of commercially ground coffee.

    Moreover, the machine can be used even in those areas that are sensitive to noise.

    This is because it does not dissipate any unnecessary noise.

    Effective Controls

    It has two control mechanisms namely the speed control and the grind adjustment respectively.

    Engaging these various control mechanisms is pretty simple.

    Its speed control employs both the gear and electronic speed reduction mechanisms.

    They self-adjust to regulate the rotation of the burr to around 500 revolutions per minute.

    It may also be manually adjusted from 405 to 495 revolutions per minute as per the specific user conditions.

    The grind adjustment feature of Baratza Virtuoso Burr Coffee Grinder may be quickly adjusted.

    From line’ for espresso to ‘coarse’ for French Press by turning the Hopper’s 40 settings.

    It’s breaking up rate ranges from 250 microns to 1,200 microns.

    Minimal Attention

    The coffee processor is designed in such a manner as to require as minimal human effort/direct intervention as can be.

    This is enabled by the existence of three main elegant features.

    The first is the sixty-second timer that has segmented graphics and which allows the seamless monitoring of the coffee pounding process.

    The second is the convenient front-mounted pulse button which makes it probable for the direct breakup of the coffee into an espresso filter basket.

    It also brings along the ability to ascertain and replicate an ideal crushing time for future grinds.

    Users do not have to be individually physically present during a coffee crushing session and may hence spend that time to pursue other chores that equally matter.

     

    Consistent Results

    As has already been alluded to earlier, the end results i.e. the quality of the final ground coffee grains is incredibly high indeed.

    The grains are exceptionally fine, soft, exceedingly smooth and of extraordinarily consistent and uniform texture.

    The grains have a distinct lack of fines throughout its pounding range.

    This stems from the fact that the burrs do have a precision mounting system which ensures a stable platform for a more accurate breakup.

    Consumers of the ground coffee are thus spared of the need to spit out some coarse particles each and every time they drink the coffee.

    Long Term Reliability

    All its key parts and components are made of especially tough and durable materials.

    The materials are quite resilient to the common elements of wear and tear such as rusting, scratches, dents, cleavages, and indeed, more besides.

    These include the sculptured metal top and base that are made of stainless steel.

    This resilience to the common elements of wear and tear makes the product extraordinarily reliable in the long run.
