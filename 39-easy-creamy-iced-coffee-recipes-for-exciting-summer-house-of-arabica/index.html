    #1 Creamy And Crunchy
     Iced Coffee Pops

    These delectable iced coffee pops are creamy with a bit of crunch. 

    And, they make the perfect dessert or treat to enjoy on a hot summer’s night.

    The recipe is easy to make and is a healthy indulgence in case you are watching your waistline.

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup medium or strong coffee, chilled
    • 2 tablespoons espresso beans
    • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
    • 1 cup raw cashews
    • 2 Tablespoons pure maple syrup
    • ½ teaspoon pure vanilla extract
    • 2 Tablespoons cacao nibs
    • 1/8 teaspoon pinch of salt (optional but I recommend)

    from withfoodandlove.com

    Directions

    1. In a blender, blend coffee, cashews, maple syrup, vanilla, and sea salt. Mix should be smooth.
    2. Grind espresso beans so that they are a fine texture.
    3. Mix ground beans and cacao nibs. Place mixture in the bottom of each popsicle mold.
    4. Pour batter into each mold and stir with the end of a spoon.
    5. Cover molds and freeze until thoroughly frozen.
    6. Enjoy this refreshing popsicle!

    My husband Jack and I love to whip up these Popsicle for a creamy, coffee night-cap.

    from LaCuochinaSopraffina.com

    #2 Classic Creamy
    Italian Coffee

    Fresh and creamy is what you get with this Italian coffee.

    With only a few ingredients, you can easily enjoy this coffee in less than 20 minutes. 

    Ingredients

    • 30 cup of espresso

    • 1.3 cup of fresh cream

    • 4 teaspoons white sugar

    Directions

    1. Brew espresso and transfer into a glass.

    2. Add sugar and stir until it melts into the espresso. Chill in refrigerator.

    3. Once cold, whip up cream and once it becomes fluffy, pour coffee continuously in a row.

    4. Place cream in the freezer for at least one hour.

    5. When cream has chilled, pour it into a cup of coffee and top it with fresh cream as well.

    This coffee is the perfect way to start or end the day.

    The fresh cream mixed with coffee creates a creamy, sweet caffeine beverage. 

    Not only that, it is super easy to make and requires only a few ingredients.

    #4 Baileys Infused
    Creamy Iced Coffee

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup whipping cream
    • ½ cup strong coffee, hot
    • 2 Tablespoons Baileys
    • 1 ½ teaspoons sugar
    • 2 ½ teaspoons dark chocolate chips, melted

    Directions

    1. Pop a glass bowl and beaters in the freezer for around 20 minutes.
    2. In another bowl, whisk hot coffee, sugar, and chocolate chips. The mixture should be smooth.
    3. Add Baileys once cool and refrigerate for another 20 minutes.
    4. Beat whipped cream until peaks appear, add in the coffee mixture, and stir slowly and carefully.
    5. Freeze for 15 minutes.
    6. Give it a stir before enjoying, dollop with whipped cream, and add chocolate on top.

    This dessert packs it all! It is creamy caffeine with a hint of chocolate.

    While it may take a few steps to prepare, the end result is worth it.

    #10 Iced Caramel Coffee
    ​ Ice Cream

    ​Ingredients

    • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
    • 1 cup Caramel Macchiato Iced Coffee
    • 2 cups whipped cream
    • ½ tsp instant coffee
    • ½ cup caramel sauce/syrup

    Directions

    1. Whisk sweetened condensed milk and iced coffee in a large bowl.
    2. Add whipped cream and stir slowly.
    3. Cover bowl and chill in the fridge for 2 hours.
    4. Stir in instant coffee once cool.
    5. Put ice cream concoction into ice cream maker.
    6. Once ice cream is made and put into a container, pour caramel sauce/syrup over.
    7. Ice cream should chill overnight or for at least 8 hours.

    This is a delicious coffee ice cream that is easy to make.

    The whipped topping creates a light ice cream that is sweet and full of caramel flavour.

    #11 Frozen Coffee Popsicles

    ​Ingredients

    • 2 cans unsweetened coconut milk
    • ¼ cup ground coffee
    • ¼ cup honey
    • 2 Tablespoon water
    • 1 teaspoon powdered gelatin

    Directions

    1. In a saucepan, add coconut milk, coffee, and honey.
      Heat over medium heat and ensure that the honey is dissolved and coconut milk comes to a simmer.
    2. Allow the mixture to sit for about 15 minutes after removing from the heat.
    3. In a bowl, add water and gelatin and allow it to sit for 10 minutes.
    4. Pour coffee mixture through a sleeve or strainer to rid any grounds.
    5. Add gelatin to the coffee milk and stir until gelatin has dissolved.
    6. Pour mixture into popsicle molds and pop into freezer for around 6 hours.

    These coffee popsicles pack a caffeine punch and make the perfect pick-me-up any time of day.

    They are easy to make and are healthy to enjoy!

    #13 Creamy Coffee Frappe

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup of iced coffee (make your own or buy. I purchased a sweet, milk iced coffee from the store).

    • Handful of crushed ice

    • Whipping cream for topping

    • Flavoured coffee creamer

    • Salted caramel sauce (buy or make your own. You can easily make with ¼ cup caramel sauce and 1 teaspoon of sea salt. Do not use table salt because the sea salt is what makes the delicious salted taste with the caramel).

    Directions

    1. In a blender, blend 1 cup of iced coffee with a large handful of crushed ice, until smooth.

    2. Whip up the whip cream and mix with ½ cup flavoured creamer of your choice.

    3. Spoon mixture over the Frappuccino.

    4. Drizzle salted caramel sauce over coffee.

    Who says you have to go to Starbucks for a salted caramel frappuccino when it is so easy to make in your own kitchen?

    The homemade salted caramel sauce combined with the sweet coffee makes for a delicious beverage to enjoy morning or night.

    #27 Very Vanilla Iced Coffee

    #36 Iced Sea Salt Coffee

    Ingredients

    • Cold brew coffee

    • Simple syrup (2 cups water and 2 cups granulated sugar)

    • Sea salt cream (1 cup heavy cream and ½ teaspoon sea salt)

    Directions

    1. For the simple syrup, in a saucepan, add water and sugar and bring to boil.

      2. Once sugar has dissolved, let cool and transfer to a container. Chill.

    2. For the sea salt cream, whisk heavy cream and salt until peaks form. Chill until use.

    3. Grab a glass and add some ice cubes.

    4. Drizzle in simple syrup and add coffee. Stir.

    5. Top with sea salt cream.

    Don’t be scared by the addition of the seal salt cream – it is amazing!
