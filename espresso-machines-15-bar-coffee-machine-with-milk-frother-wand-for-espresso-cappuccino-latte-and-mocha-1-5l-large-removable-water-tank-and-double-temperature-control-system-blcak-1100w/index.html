    Precautions for the use of Espresso Machine

    Milk Foaming Precautions

    You can adjust the amount of steam according to personal preference.

    For better milk foam effect, it is recommended to fill the pitcher with milk and insert the frothing nozzle into milk about 1 cm.

    Should Not Exceed the MAX Capacity

    Remove the detachable tank and fill it with desired water.

    Note: The water level should not exceed the MAX mark in the tank, or the water will leak out the water tank.

    Coffee Powder Precautions

    Do not put coffee powder which is too fine into the funnel, or the powder will leak out the funnel.

    Do not exceed the MAX mark on the funnel. A spoonful of coffee spoon is about 7g powder. When making coffee, you can put 2 tablespoons of powder.

    Do not push the coffee powder too hard, or the water will leak from the edge of the funnel.

    Removable Cup Holder

    The bottom of the machine is a movable cup holder. Not just small coffee cups, large coffee cups also can use this machine.

    You can remove the cup holder at the bottom of the machine, if your cup is too tall/large.
