    What You Should Consider Before Buying a Coffee Maker

    Before buying any coffee maker, there are valuable things to consider.

     

    If you are seeking out a coffee pot that will brew delicious cups of coffee every time, then it is important to do your research.

    Related: 13 Coffee Brewing Tips

     Luckily, I have helped you out with that. Before buying one, such as the Cusinart DCC-3200 Coffee Maker, consider the following:

    • Brewing temperature
    • How much you consume on a daily basis
    • Special features (Do you want all the extras?)
    • Size and weight
    • How easy is it to clean?
    • Expensive parts?

    While you may think that a coffee maker is a small investment, it is important to consider all aspects before buying. 

    For example, if you want a deliciously rich cup of coffee each time, check for a coffee pot that reaches high temperatures.

    Or, if you guzzle a lot of coffee, buying a machine that produces up to at least 12 cups is ideal.

    Presenting Cuisinart DCC-3200

    The Cuisinart DCC-3200 coffee maker is a 14-cup maker.

    It has superior production performance, convenience, and taste according to the consumer report.

    This means that it is possible to make delicious coffee with this device.

    I am pleased because it is programmable to a specific temperature or time, the strength is controllable. 

    It is an overall stylish maker!

    (if that is something of concern to you! I always like an attractive looking coffee machine to sit on my counter, although my husband could care less) 

    Compared to similar products on the market, such as the stainless steel HAMSWAN AD-103 coffee maker, the DCC 3200 of Cuisinart brews 4 more cups and claims to reach temperatures over 195 degrees Fahrenheit. Capable of brewing up to 205 degrees Fahrenheit.

    Check out more on my Pros and Cons list below

    Pros

    • Reaches high temperatures
    • Easy to clean and alerts when cleaning is needed
    • Holds 71 oz.

    Cons

    • Steam emits which can be damaging to counters if placed near
    • Loud
    • Many controls which can be confusing

    Special Features

    1. Brew temperature

    One of the most significant features on the Cuisinart DCC-3200 coffee maker is its ability to reach up to 205 degrees Fahrenheit.

    This means that you can enjoy a steaming hot cup of coffee every single morning.

    Not only that! It has an adjustable warmer temperature control that allows you to be in control of your coffee’s temperature!

    (yes, you heard me right – no more heating your coffee up in the microwave or having to blow on it until it’s cool).

    2. Stylish buttons 

    The next best feature on this Cuisinart coffee maker is the stylish buttons that grace the front.

    There are 9 buttons:

    • Clean 
    • Bold 
    • Brew Off/Auto Off 
    • Prog 
    • Auto On 
    • 1-4 
    • Hour 
    • Minute 
    • Tone 
    • and Carafe Temperature 

    There is even a nice digital clock in the center.

    These buttons are helpful and convenient when deciding on how I like to make my coffee.

    3. Sleek Design and Material 

    The stainless-steel material combined with the sleek design makes this an attractive coffee machine to leave out on your countertop.

    Stainless steel is a sturdy material. 

    So, you do not have to worry about deterioration, as well as it is easy to clean if coffee splatters.

    ​Related: Cleaning A Coffee Maker

    The glass carafe has a dark appearance but it too is stylish and attractive.

     

    The only thing you may want to ensure is that you are careful when handling the carafe.

    Because if it drops it can shatter or break.

    4. Convenient Filters 

    I am happy to discover that there is a permanent filter and water filter in this maker.

    Because it saves money in the long run on buying filters.

    Additionally, you do not have to spend the time changing filters (each time you prepare coffee.)

    5. Small Batch Option

    The Cuisinart DCC-3200 also has options of brewing small batches of coffee.

    Let’s face it!

    Sometimes you may need small amount coffee, especially if you are by yourself. 

    Therefore, take advantage of the small-batch setting.

    And produce a personal sized amount of coffee.

     

    Keep in mind, you have to pour it by hand, as it does not have a Dispenser-style pour like a Keurig.

    Competitor Coffee Makers

    Across the board, DCC-3200 Cuisinart is superior compared to its competitors. 

    Below I put together a comprehensive list of the leading competitors.

    I placed a fair comparison on my end.

    ​1. Hamilton Beach 46205

    Similar to the Cuisinart DCC-3200 coffee maker, Hamilton Beach 46205 is stainless steel and comes with a glass carafe. 

    It comes equipped with a clock that is completely programmable.

    Has an easily accessible base that allows for reaching the water reservoir in a pinch.

    It does not reach as many cups produced as the Cuisinart DCC-3200.

    But it is able to make 12 cups. I think that is a good amount. 

    When it is time to pour, the 46205 Hamilton Beach is equipped with a mess-free pouring spout.

    The non-stick warming plate is good enough for easy serving. That does not require clean-up. 

    Additionally, the company has made it so easy that you can enjoy a cup of coffee (without waiting for the entire batch to finish).

    Thanks to the automatic pause and serve function.

    Whether you are in the mood for a bold cup or regular one, you can conveniently have both with Hamilton Beach 46204 maker.

    If it is time to refill the brew basket, you can easily swing it out and use the release button.

    Hamilton Beach’s coffee maker appears to be simple to use (much like the Cuisinart DCC-3200) 

    It is affordable, but a little pricey compared to the other leading coffee makers on the market.

    Gusto Genio

    This unique looking machine is able to prepare hot or cold coffee drinks using a single-serve system.

    So, whether you are in the mood for iced coffee, hot lattes, cappuccinos, or original coffees, this device can do it all.

    The lights on the coffee maker light up green, which is a stylish feature. 

    The great thing about the DeLonghi Nescafe Dolce Gusto Genio is –

    the users can conveniently customize their own coffee strength using the buttons on the front of the device.

    3. Hamilton Beach 49980A

    The stainless-steel look is repeated with this Hamilton Beach Coffee Maker, and the glass carafe adds a stylish touch the overall appearance.

    It comes in a variety of ways to enjoy: 14 oz. travel mug size or 12-cup carafe style, but again, it does not stand up to our featured product’s 14-cup ability.

     

    That being said, it does offer a bold or regular brew, therefore appealing to a variety of coffee consumers.

    When you go to pour the Hamilton Beach 49980A, you can switch between the single-serve option or full brew-basket. 

    Additionally, there is a sleek control panel and display, and users can make coffee up to 24 hours or use the programmable timer with a 2-hour automatic shut-off. 

    However, do not worry because even after you shut it off, coffee stays hot thanks to the keep-hot plate that comes with the maker. 

    Hamilton Beach’s 49980A Coffee Maker can be purchased for a cheaper price. That makes it an affordable coffee maker.

    ​4. Cuisinart DCC-2600

    In the same family as the Cuisinart DCC-3200, the Cuisinart DCC-2600 is the same in that it makes 14-cups of coffee. The machine itself is made out of stainless-steel and comes with an easy-to-clean glass carafe.

    Similar to the Cuisinart DCC-3200, this coffee maker comes equipped with a permanent gold tone filter and charcoal water filter, and this allows for superior brewing and taste.

     

    When you are done pouring your coffee, there is a convenient heater plate with adjustable temperature there for you to use to keep your coffee warm.

    Additionally, this coffee maker comes equipped with a 0 – 4-hour automatic shut-off.

    It can be purchased for slightly less than the DCC-3200 Coffee Maker

    5. Bunn BX Velocity Brew

    Bunn BXB Velocity Brew can produce up to 10-cups and is easy to use. One quick glance at this coffee maker and you will see style, modern design, and convenience all in one package. The machine is mainly black but has added touches of stainless-steel which makes it attractive.

     

    Users also no longer have to worry about uneven pouring of coffee or dripping thanks to the drip-free decanter and unique spray-head which takes care of those issues. And, the brewing temperature reaches a toasty level of 200 degrees Fahrenheit.

    If, for some reason, you have issues with your Bunn BX Velocity, it does come with a 3-year limited warranty. This means, you can buy with confidence.

    ​6. Keurig K40 Elite Brewing System

    Experience a personal coffee experience with the Keurig K40 Elite Brewing System.

    This is ideal for those who live by themselves or are the only coffee drinker in the house.

    Because you can make one cup of coffee at a time.

    This coffee maker has a convenient water reservoir on the left side.

    So you do not have to pull it out to reach it at the back.

    Additionally, it is able to prepare 6 oz., 8 oz., and 10 oz. size coffees and the water heats fast. 

    When you purchase the Keurig K40 Elite Brewing System, you receive a K-Cup variety pack, and a sleek machine that brews great tasting coffee.

    There is a removable drip tray that is simple to empty and clean out when it gets dirty.

    If this machine is what you are looking for, you can easily purchase one on an affordable price.

    While it is a small investment, it is an easy to use machine that gives you delicious coffee each time in under a minute.

    This product differs from the Cuisinart DCC-3200 Coffee Maker in that it heats up faster but it only brews personal sized coffees rather than an entire batch.

    Price wise, it is similar, but you do have to purchase K-cups to use in the machine versus coffee grounds.
