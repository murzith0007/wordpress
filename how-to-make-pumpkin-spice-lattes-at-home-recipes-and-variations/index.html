    Why to Use Pumpkin
    Spice In Coffee?

    Pumpkin Spice is traditionally a blend of cinnamon, nutmeg, and clove, three spices that offer a warm, festive taste in each sip.

    Pumpkin Spice is perfect in coffee because it blends well with almost any strength.

    Additionally, it is offers just the right amount of sweetness that cuts out any bitter flavors from the coffee bean.

    Adding Pumpkin Spice to your coffee is also the perfect beverage to enjoy on a crisp fall morning.

    Pumpkin Spice Latte History

    Pumpkin Spice has a long history in the world of coffee. Its development began in 2003 and it appeared in Starbucks stores in 2004.

    The recipe of the PSL went under many changes to get it to the taste and consistency it is today.

    It started out being a recipe that contained chemicals and artificial flavors, and since 2015, it contains no more artificial flavors.

    It is easy to see how this delicious latte spice grew to become the popular option it is now!

    Why Starbucks’s Pumpkin Spice Latte is so Popular?

    In the past couple decades, the Starbucks franchise has grown increasingly popular. So it makes sense that when they make a drink people scientifically love, they go crazy.

    That’s right – science is the reason behind our love for all things pumpkin spice. It may sound crazy, but it gives a nostalgia to the fall season.

    The aroma triggers the happy memories associated with the scent to flood your brain. That’s why we can’t get enough of it!

    Why Starbucks’s Pumpkin Spice Latte is Better Than Ever?

    Since it was made in 2003, the recipe hadn’t changed much. Back in 2015, however, the company made a rather large change to the drink.

     

    That year they added real pumpkin to the list of ingredients. This really gives the drink that extra pumpkin flavor. If we weren’t hooked before, we definitely are now!

    When Does Starbucks Pumpkin Spice Latte Come Out?

    It seems every year the Starbucks Pumpkin Spice Latte has a different release date.

     

    Last year in 2018, it came out at the end of August, but it has also come out in September in the past.

    Typically, you can expect the PSL to come out towards the end of August just in time for September.

    Brands Offering Pumpkin Spice Latte

    Dunkin Donuts

    Dunkin Donuts is a major coffee company that has their own version of a PSL. The PSL they offer looks like a regular latte, but it offers a sweet taste of pumpkin.

    Dunkin Donuts uses a syrup to flavor their coffee to taste like pumpkin. Because of the popularity of the brand, this PSL does get a lot of love from customers.

     

    However, it is said to be a little too sweet in its aftertaste. That being said, Dunkin Donuts offers it for a fairly reasonable price compared to other large chain coffee shops.

    McDonalds

    Being already a leader in the fast food world only meant the obvious come out with their own version of the PSL.

    McDonalds claims to freshly brew their coffee that the latte is made with and blend it with a satisfying aroma of cinnamon, pumpkin, and whole or non-fat milk.

    It comes with a dollop of whipped cream on top, and can be purchased for a low price compared to its competitors.

    The calories in a PSL at McDonalds is 270 for a small, so it teeters on the low-high range.

    Gloria Jean’s Coffee Company

    Gloria Jean’s Pumpkin Spice Latte is the perfect blend of pumpkin, cinnamon, and nutmeg with a medium roast coffee bean.

    The result is a smooth and sweet taste that brings an explosion of fall in your mouth.

    Well, you be the judge of that. However, Gloria Jean’s Coffee Company prides itself in a premium Pumpkin Spice coffee that you can mix with any milk of your choice.

    Compared to other lattes from other companies, Gloria Jean’s is bolder in taste rather than overly sweet.

    Peet’s Coffee and Tea

    Lactose-intolerant and Vegan consumers unite! Peet’s Coffee and Tea’s Pumpkin Spice Latte is entirely dairy-free from the drink itself to the delicious toppings.

    The latte is crafted with espresso, pumpkin, and the usual spices, offering a sweet but not too sweet PSL.

    On top, coconut milk is whipped and dusted with pumpkin spice to create the perfect little topping on your drink!

    Not every coffee company offers a dairy-free PSL, so Peet’s Coffee and Tea can pride themselves in the fact that they do offer one!

    This latte is quickly becoming a popular choice, as the brand is already a favorite of many coffee drinkers.

    Tim Horton’s

    Tim Horton’s Pumpkin Spice Latte comes in a hot version and a cold version.

    Tim Horton’s isn’t too open about what they use to create their PSL, but it has a sweet pumpkin taste dolloped with a generous size of whipped cream on top and caramel drizzle.

    The price of this PSL is average, as you are able to purchase a PSL from another chain coffee business for more.

    However, Tim Horton’s grew its popularity in Canada and is now an American company, so there have been changes to some of their drinks.

    The PSL still remains a fall favorite to dedicated Timmies lovers.

    Caribou Coffee

    Step to the side Pumpkin Spice Latte because Caribou Coffee has something even more delicious: Pumpkin Pie Latte.

    That’s right, you can order a pie inspired latte. You can enjoy this PPL iced, hot, or in a Cooler version.

    In their PPL, espresso, steamed milk, and pumpkin syrup are all blended to create a not too sweet but just the right amount of pumpkin latte.

    Caribou Coffee is a popular coffee business that can proudly say their Pumpkin Pie Latte is a real fall hit.

    Panera Bread

    Already a popular business, of course Panera Bread had to release their own Pumpkin Spice Latte.

    They keep it simple by only mixing espresso, milk, pumpkin syrup, whipped cream, and salted caramel drizzle.

    Compared to other coffee chains who make PSL, Panera Bread has done something slightly different with theirs: adding in caramel drizzle.

    The caramel offers a different enjoyable taste while you are sipping on pumpkin infused coffee.

    Most of Panera Bread’s items, including lattes, are affordable and considered average when up against chains like Starbucks or Peet’s.

    Einstein Bros Bagels

    The Pumpkin Spice Latte from Einstein Bros Bagels combines coffee, milk, and pumpkin syrup…pretty simple, right?

    While it does use easy ingredients, the taste of the PSL from Einstein Bros Bagels may not be everyone’s cup-of-tea (or coffee!).

    This is because their PSL is on the sweet side, perhaps artificially sweet side. That being said, it is delicious and pairs well with any one of their breakfast bagels or muffins.

     

    Einstein Bros Bagel’s offers affordable prices to customers, so it may be a place you want to check out when fall arrives.

    How to Make Pumpkin Spice Latte At Home?

    As good as a nice pumpkin spice latte from Starbuck can taste, it’s not always friendly on the wallet. The good news is, there are ways to do it in your own kitchen.

    Making your drink at home also allows you to customize it just like at Starbucks. You can get your pumpkin spice fix many different ways for cheap.

    All You Need

    # Ingredients:

    • Pumpkin Purée 2 tablespoons

    • Pumpkin Spice 1/2 teaspoon + garnish

    • Ground Black Pepper

    • Sugar 2 tablespoons

    • Vanilla Extract 2 tablespoons

    • Milk 2 cups

    • Espresso 1-2 shots

    • Heavy Cream 1/4 cup

    # Equipment:

    • Mixer/Whisk to whip cream

    • Saucepan

    • Wooden spoon

    • Whisk

    • Blender

    • Espresso maker

    #3 Instructions

    Heat the Pumpkin and Spices: 

    ​For 2 minutes, heat pumpkin, pumpkin spice, and black pepper in a saucepan over medium heat, stirring continuously.

    ​Stir in the Sugar:

    ​This should create a syrup consistency that is thick and is bubbling.

    Warm the Milk: 

    ​Stir the vanilla in with the milk and warm without boiling.

    Blend: 

    ​Blend the milk and pumpkin mixtures in a blender until frothy.

    ​Mix The Drinks: 

    ​Combine the espresso and milk/pumpkin mixture.

    ​Slimmer: 

    ​If you want a more slim version trying using 2% milk instead of whole.

    ​Garnish and Enjoy: 

    ​Add your choice of pumpkin pie spice, nutmeg, and cinnamon and relax!

    Pumpkin Spice Latte Hacks

    • Add Hazelnut Syrup: This makes it taste even more like pumpkin pie!

    • Put it On Ice: Same great fall taste in a summer drink!

    • Add Some Chocolate Chips: Everything’s better with a little chocolate.

    • Substitute Coconut Milk: Another way to try a summer edition of the famous autumn coffee.

    • No Espresso: If you don’t need the caffeine, ditch it. You may be surprised how much you like the drink without it.

    • Spicy Chai PSL: If you really like your chai, add two pumps of pumpkin syrup instead of doing a regular pumpkin spice latte.

    • Add Salted Caramel: Want to add a little sweet that’s not too overwhelming?

    Try a pump of caramel and a dash of salt.

    • Keep it Simple: If you’re not into all the extra coffee additions, get a pump of pumpkin spice syrup in your regular coffee.

    • Too Sweet: If the drink you get is a lot sweeter than you expected, don’t be hesitant to add a little extra cinnamon or nutmeg.

    Uses Of Pumpkin Spice Latte In Foods

    Pumpkin spice is a common ingredient used in food items like loaves, cookies, muffins, puddings, and even cakes.

    It is a spice that blends well with others and mixes perfectly with dairy products.

    Pumpkin Spice Lattes have quickly become not only a beverage, but a food item as well because you can add the ingredients into solid foods.

    For example, you can create PSL Muffins or Bagels. It is super easy!


    Pumpkin Spice Latte Benefits

    Health Benefits

    Benefit Nutrition Per Serving

    Pumpkin Spice Lattes can be enjoyed in a small version to keep tabs on the sugar content.

    In fact, a small PSL is not harmful to the body and can easily range from the low 200’s to 300 calories per serving.

    It is recommended to get a small size when ordering from a commercial coffee store because the sugar content could be high.

    Anti-Inflammatory Properties

    Pumpkin is a warming spice, similar to turmeric! Usually, a pumpkin spice blend will contain nutmeg which can actually mask pain.

    As a result, pumpkin is a good spice to consume if you have arthritis or body pain.


    Good dose of Fiber

    Pumpkin is fibrous and that helps keep your digestive system moving and blood pressure regular. The addition of pumpkin to any diet can keep your body happy!

    Promotes a Strong Immune System

    Pumpkin is a good source of zinc which is actually proven to reduce the length of colds.

    This is a great excuse to enjoy more PSL’s, especially if you are someone who catches colds easy!


    Other Benefits

    Encourages a healthy menstrual cycle. This is especially important if you are experiencing irregular cycles or painful periods. The pumpkin is also relaxing so it can comfort an uncomfortable time.

    Anti-cancer properties. Just make sure that when you are enjoying your PSL, you cut-out the addition of white sugar, as this has been shown to fuel cancer growth.

    Healthy dose of vitamin C, antioxidants and iron. These things are all important in building a strong immune system and promoting healthy blood.
