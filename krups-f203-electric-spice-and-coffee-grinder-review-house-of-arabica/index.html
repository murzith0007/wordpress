    Things To Consider 
    (Before Buying KRUPS F203)

    If you’re like me, you may not have all the time in the world to grind your coffee beans.

    So of course, you want something that is quick and easy.

    Also, not many people can afford to go out and buy a new grinder every week because yours decides it is time to retire after only a few uses.

    So now what we’re looking for is a grinder that’s not only quick and easy, but also durable.

    But if you’re one of those people that would rather grind beans the old fashioned way without electronics, this grinder may not be for you.

    KRUPS F203 Electric Spice
    And Coffee Grinder Review

    The KRUPS F203 is a grinder for not only coffee, but spices as well.

    It is also one of the #1 top selling blade grinders on many Amazon.

    Now, if you are an anti-blade person, then obviously you may not want to check this one out.

    This is more for the people that don’t want to spend hundreds of dollars on an expensive burr grinder.

    KRUPS F 203: Pros

    So first, let’s talk about the pros of this grinder.

    It has a large coffee bean capacity.

    The grinder can hold up to 12 cups of coffee beans so you can grind a whole pot of coffee within a few minutes.

    It has a safety switch.

    For those of you, like myself, have children and want to grab anything and everything.

    You may worry that they might try to get curious about the grinder because it has sharp blades.

    But no worries!

    The blades don’t spin if the lid isn’t locked into place, so the little fingers are safe.

    It is inexpensive.

    You might be thinking that with it being a “fancy” grinder, that it is a not-so-fancy price.

    It is actually one of the cheaper grinders, only being in the low range.

    So you get more for your money.

    KRUPS F 203: Cons

    Now of course, with pros comes cons.

    Nothing is ever perfect so here’s a couple downsides to this grinder.

    Being a blade grinder, you may not get the full flavor of coffee.

    Blade grinders are notorious for expelling heat while grinding.

    And that affects the natural flavoring of the beans.

    Very limited control of grind size. The blades are raised just a little.

    So, if you’re looking for a very fine grind, you probably won’t get it with this.

    It is designed for more coarse grinds.

    Hassle to clean

    Some parts cannot be removed. So it’s a little annoying!

    You have to get every crevice of the grinder when you’re cleaning it. 

    Sometimes, you might miss a spot ! And it may affect the flavor of the next round of grinding.

    Features And Benefits

    Now that we’ve discussed the pros and cons of this grinder, let’s get into what it can do for you.

    1. Safety Lid

    As I mentioned earlier, this grinder has a safety lid for those of us who have kids, or those of us that might just be plain clumsy.

    The lid must be fully closed in order for it to grind.

    So there’s no need to worry about any little ones getting curious about what’s inside while it’s grinding.

    Now I will add, you should never leave your grinder unoccupied anyway.

    2. Stainless Steel Blades​

    ​To ensure durability when grinding, this grinder has a strong and sturdy stainless steel blade.

    You won’t have to worry about if your grinder will come into contact with a stronger bean and end up not being able to grind it properly, or even completely breaking.

    3. Speed Grinding

    ​As I previously pointed out, the grinder can hold up to 12 cups of beans.

    Adding onto that, it grinds 3 ounces in a matter of seconds.

    Now if you choose to grind all 12 cups that it holds, it might take a couple minutes instead of a few seconds.

    But 3 ounces at once within seconds is definitely not a bad deal.

    Coffee Grinder Alternatives 

    Now we will talk about some other options if you don’t think this one is right for you.

    ​Epica Electric Coffee & Spice Grinder

    This grinder is very similar to the KRUPS F203.

    One of the differences is it only has a 2.5 ounce capacity.

    Also, it has 250 watt motor as opposed to the KRUP’s 200 watt motor.

    This grinder is a little cheaper range as well for those that don’t want to spend the money on the KRUPS F203.

    ​Hamilton Beach 80335R Fresh-Grind Coffee Grinder 

    This grinder is almost exactly the same as the KRUPS F203.

    The main difference is the size.

    This grinder is a tad bit bigger, not even really enough to notice.

    However, it is also the cheaper of the two.

    If you’re interested in the KRUPS but you would rather save about ten dollars, this might be your best bet.

    >> Check Price on Amazon <<

    ​Mr. Coffee 12 Cup Coffee Grinder
    (with Multi Settings)

    This grinder is a little different. It has a few more features than the KRUPS F203.

    To start, it has options where you can choose what grind size you want.

    It automatically grinds to that desired size, ranging from fine to medium to coarse.

    So you won’t have to just look through the top and figure out what size you have.

    Also, it has a self-cleaning function that sweeps the walls of the chamber.

    This is definitely a good pick for those that aren’t big on cleaning grinders, and you will also save few dollars, right?

    >> Check Price on Amazon <<

    ​Quiseen One-Touch Electric Coffee Grinder 

    This grinder is about the same price, but the chamber is smaller than the KRUPS F203.

    It only holds about 7 cups of coffee beans and grinds 2.5 ounces at a time.

    So if you want more for your money, this one might not be the best pick for you.

    >> Check Price on Amazon <<

    ​KitchenAid BCG211OB Blade Coffee and Spice Grinder 

    This grinder is more expensive than the KRUPS F203, and also the other alternatives I have mentioned.

    However, it holds 12 cups of coffee beans and grinds 4 ounces at once.

    So you will most likely grind the 12 cups in this grinder than you will using the KRUPS.

    This grinder also bigger than the KRUPS F203.

    For example, it is wider and taller.

    Which would probably explain why it grinds more at one time.

    If you want a grinder that’s bigger, you may have to spend a few more bucks, but this one might be perfect.
