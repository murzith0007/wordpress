\n At Real Good Coffee Company we are a small team who pride ourselves on making real good coffee. It’s that simple. Good coffee shouldn’t come with premiums for exotic names and fancy packaging. We promise not to distract you with gimmicks. once you taste the coffee you’ll be impressed.\n <\/p>\n

\n Donut Shop Whole Bean Coffee\n <\/p>\n

\n Real Good Coffee Co Donut Shop whole bean coffee is for the purist looking for an effortless blend of nostalgia and flavor that doesn’t disappoint. This medium roast, extra bold coffee brings together our best beans for a sweet and smooth flavor from the first taste to the bottom of your cup. Take a sip of this classic coffee and get transported to an old fashioned donut shop with the aroma and flavor of brown sugar and hazelnut, and add a hint of plum for a little sweetness. Our Donut Shop medium roast coffee is perfect with your morning pastry, but smooth enough to drink on it’s own. If you’re looking for a perfect daily blend, this is the coffee for you.\n <\/p>\n

\n Sustainable Medium Roast Whole Bean Coffee\n <\/p>\n

\n Made with 100% Arabica, coffee beans and recyclable materials, this light roast coffee is kosher, and has no additives, preservatives or artificial ingredients. The coffee beans are roasted fresh in Seattle, USA, and are suitable for use in any coffee machine including Aeropress, pour over, drip machines, espresso makers, and moka pots.\n <\/p>\n

\n How To Make Our Donut Shop Coffee:\n <\/p>\n<\/p>\n

Prior to using our medium roast whole bean coffee, grind the beans to either a medium or coarse grind depending on the type of coffee machine you are using.
\nDrip Pot- Add 2 Tbsp (10 grams) of medium grind coffee for every 6 oz.of filtered water.
\nFrench Press- 9 Tbsp (55 grams) of coarse grind coffee for a 34 oz.french press. Steep for 4 minutes before plunging.
\nPour Over- 4 Tbsp (22 grams) of medium grind coffee for every 12 oz.of filtered water. Pour Slowly.<\/p>\n
